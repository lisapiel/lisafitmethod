Why this exists

I learned the hard way.
You don't have to.

A few years ago I was going to the gym consistently, doing everything I thought was right. I wasn't. I was skipping warm-ups, ignoring mobility, following random workouts with no real structure behind them. I thought more weight and harder work was the answer.

Then my back gave out. For almost a year I lived with serious pain. Getting out of bed was uncomfortable. Sitting at a desk was uncomfortable. The gym was gone. That year taught me more about training than everything before it combined.

I had to relearn everything from scratch. I became a certified personal trainer because I needed to actually understand what I'd been doing wrong. I rebuilt my body the right way. Stronger than I'd ever been. Pain-free. And I've stayed that way.

This program is everything I wish someone had given me before I started.
